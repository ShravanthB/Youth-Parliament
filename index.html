About Me

I am a Year 8 student running for Slough Youth Elections because I believe students deserve to be heard, supported, and proud of their school. I am confident, approachable, and ready to represent you.

Student Voice

Every student has ideas, concerns, and suggestions that deserve to be heard and recognised, and I want to make sure they are. School is more than the lessons; it's about creating a community where everyone feels included and valued. If I am voted into youth Parliament, I will listen to your ideas, take them seriously, and bring them straight to the people who can make a difference. Whether it's a suggestion for an event to celebrate someone or something, an improvement to school life, or simply something you want to share, I want to hear it and make sure it counts. Remember, "Voice doesn't end at the classroom door".

Mental Health and Wellbeing

Imagine a school where everyone feels understood, supported, and able to be themselves. That's the school I want to help create. Mental health and wellbeing is not something to be overlooked, for this reason, I am going to make sure that students have the tools, support and spaces to manage stress, share their experiences, and feel connected. Together, we can build a community that listens, cares and looks after one another; a place where everyone feels valued and supported.

School Environment

Our school should be a place where everyone can learn, relax, and enjoy their time. I want to make sure we have the right resources, at the right times. I want to push for upgrades for nicer common areas, quiet zones for work and also creative spaces to broaden skills and hobbies to create larger and wider skillsets. Together, we can create an environment where everyone feels safe, fun and creative. I'll listen to your ideas and work to turn them into spaces that make a real difference every day, without compromising on knowledge intake.

Communication

I believe in clear, honest updates. I will keep you informed about what is happening and what progress is being made towards our commmon goal.
